About Me

Hello! I'm Sean. I am a wearer of many hats. Currently, I am a user interface / user experience designer (UI/UX) for Northwestern's Feinberg School of Medicine. I specialize in web and mobile interfaces. I am a graduate of University of Illinois in Chicago and more recently, the Startup Institute, located at 1871. With over 3 years of working with technology based startups and research studies, I now aspire to be a real world (irl) unicorn -- a designer + developer.

When I'm not working, I am vigorously nerding out to video games, mashing different genres of electronic music on the decks, or cooking some damn delicious food.
